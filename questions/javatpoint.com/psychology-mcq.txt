CodeBlock:

Options:
0 . flashbulb memory
1 . serial position effect
2 . encoding specificity effects
3 . none of the above
4 . 
Correct Answer: 1

Explanation: The serial-position effect can be defined as a tendency in which the accuracy of recalling the first and last items in the list is greater, and the accuracy of recalling the items in the middle is worst.The Serial-position effect involves two concepts that are the primacy effect and the recency effect. In the recency effect, the accuracy of recalling the items at the end of the list is greater than the items in the middle.
